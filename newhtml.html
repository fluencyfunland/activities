Colour In

Colour in exercises offer you and your child a great opportunity to work through self expression and colour recognition skills. This site offers you a variety of images to colour in and have fun with.

Instructions

Select a picture to colour in from the main menu. You can then choose which colour you wish to use from the crayons located on the right hand side of the screen. Once you have selected a colour, click a section of the picture to fill it with that colour.
